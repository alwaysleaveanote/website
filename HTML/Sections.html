Headlines

There are 6 headline options < h1 > through < h6 > which create headlines in different sizes.
They are separated by one line of white space.

Articles

the < article > tag is used to define sections of the page devoted to an "article" (blog post, newspaper, etc)
Articles have a more general < section > tag (with id attribute to give a name to the section you are defining) which
breaks the article up into more general sections (e.g page, chapter, etc). Neither of these tags are in line.
There is a white line between each different section in an article.

adding figures to an article is the same as adding the figure to your webpage, except you enclose it in the
< figure > (not inline) tag. You can add a caption using the < figcaption > tag. Adding hyperlinks is also the same
as adding links, except you enclose it in the < nav > (not inline) tag. Both of these tags have id attributes because
they are considered sections of the article
