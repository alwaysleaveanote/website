2013 Seattle Crime Rates:

A data visualization of crime within the Seattle area in the year 2013.

Click an area to see a time density plot by type of crime and a map of crime for that specific area.
You can also toggle checkboxes to show the crime density for a specific type crime througout all of seattle.

This project is hosted on a relatively slow server, so it may take a while for the maps to load - be patient
